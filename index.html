About Us:

Mosque Aisha's location is very convenient and close to Niagara Falls and offers tourists visiting the Niagara Region easy access to perform prayers at a facility so close to the Falls. Mosque Aisha offers five daily prayers including Friday prayer. We have Quran classes 4 days a week.
